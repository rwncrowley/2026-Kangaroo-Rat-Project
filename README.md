# RC-2026-Kangaroo-Rat-Project
This data is the data collected so far from Rowan Crowley's kangaroo rat senior thesis project.
The goal of the thesis is to collect data from contemporary Dipodomys heermanni goldmani populations 
in  Fort Ord Natural Reserve, Marina CA, Strathearn Ranch Natural Reserve,
East of Hollister, CA and Hastings Natural History Reservation, 
Carmel Valley, CA in order to see if Kangaroo rats have intraspecies habitat 
dependent variation.

When small mammal trapping at Hastings Natural history reservation which has historical had two sympatric species of kangaroo rats
the Narrow-faced kangaroo rat Dipodomys Venustus and the Heerman's kangaroo rat Dipodyms heermanni.
